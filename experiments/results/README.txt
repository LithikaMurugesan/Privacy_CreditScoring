Results of FL Credit Scoring experiments are saved here.

Files:
  - {timestamp}_comparison.csv   — accuracy/AUC/epsilon per training mode
  - {timestamp}_comparison.png   — bar chart comparison plot
  - best_model.pt                — most recently trained model weights
  - {experiment_name}_client_log.csv  — per-bank per-round metrics
  - {experiment_name}_global_log.csv  — global model metrics per round
